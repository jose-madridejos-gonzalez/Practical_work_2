Degree

A general overwiew of the degree and its subjects (only the mainstream ones)

Subject Year(s) Field Quick explanation
Calculus 1st, 2nd, 3rd & 4th Mathematics Explore through calculation how mathematics interacts with human activity and as a tool for decision-making, and seeking relationships. (this covers calculus and numeric calculus)
General mathematics 1st & 2nd Mathematics This covers all the subjects that explain an aspect of maths that is needed in order to continue studying in this degree. This includes: Algebra, Mathematic Logic, Discrete Mathematics and Differential Equations
Programming 1st & 2nd Computer science The Introduction to Programming course introduces students to basic programming concepts. The course focuses on problem solving, building programmes that solve simple problems.
Fundamentals of Computer Engineering (FCE) 1st Computer science Before knowing about Computer Engineering subjects, learning the fundamentals in crucial for it.
Computer Engineering 1st, 2nd, 3rd & 5th Computer Science This covers all subjects that specialice on any aspects of the engineering behind computers and operative systems (this includes Software Engineering, Software Designing Operative Systems and Analisis & Design of Information Systems)
Proyects 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th & 5th Computer Science & Mathematics At the end of every year, it is mandatory to do a final proyect, so this row includes Proyects, Integrative Proyect and TFG
